(Español) Diccionario de las variables. Todas las variables monetarias están en dólares estadounidenses:

Credit Number: Es el identificador único que se le da a cada crédito.
Region: Región geográfica, de acuerdo a la administración del Banco Mundial, a la que pertenece el país solicitante. 
Country: Nombre del país que solicita el crédito.
Credit Status: Estado en el que se encuentra el crédito a la fecha del corte del período: diciembre 2021.
Service Charge: Tasa de interés actual o tasa aplicada en su momento al crédito.
Currency of Commitment: Moneda en la que se maneja el crédito.
Project ID: Identificador único del proyecto al que se destinan los recursos.
Project Name: Nombre del proyecto al que se destinan los recursos.
Original Principal Amount: Monto original solicitado.
Cancelled Amount: Porción del monto original que no es otorgado. 
Undisbursed Amount: Monto a otorgarse con el que aún dispone el prestatario para utilizarse.
Disbursed Amount: Monto que se ha otorgado al prestatario.
Repair to IDA: Monto liquidado por parte del país deudor.
First Repayment Date: Fecha para comenzar a pagar la deuda.
Last Repayment Date: Última fecha para cubrir la deuda.
Agreement Signing Date: Fecha en que el prestatario y el Banco firmal el contrato del préstamo.

(English) Diccionary of variables. All monetary variables are in USD:
Credit Number: It is the unique identifier given to each credit.
Region: Geographical region, according to the World Bank administration, to which the requesting country belongs.
Country: Name of the country to which loan has been issued.
Credit Status: Status in which the  credit is found on the cut-off date of the period: December 2021.
Service Charge: Current interest rate or rate applied at the time to the credit.
Currency of Commitment: Currency in which the credit is handled.
Project ID: Unique identifier of the project to which the resources are destined.
Project Name: Name of the project to which the resources are destined.
Original Principal Amount: Original amount requested.
Canceled Amount: Portion of the original amount that is not granted.
Undisbursed Amount: Amount to be granted with which the borrower still has to use.
Disbursed Amount: Amount that has been granted to the borrower.
Repair to IDA: Amount settled by the debtor country.
First Repayment Date: Date to start paying the debt.
Last Repayment Date: Last date to cover the debt.
Agreement Signing Date: Date on which the borrower and the Bank signed the loan contract.
